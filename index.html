A MESSAGE FROM MAYOR MIKE MOORE

Mayor Mike Moore

Mayor Mike Moore, August 2024

Welcome to the City of Jeffersonville! Whether you have lived here your whole life or are just stopping by, we hope you see just how much we have to offer.

New businesses and families continue to choose Jeffersonville as their home. I got into public service with hopes of improving our local economy and our neighborhoods. I’m so proud of where Jeffersonville is today.

Check out our downtown by taking a stroll across the Big Four Bridge and visit our Farmers Market. Stop by Chapel Lake Park for a picnic or afternoon of fishing. Experience the variety of shopping and restaurants on Veterans Parkway.

The options are endless and there’s a reason there’s a feeling felt #onlyINjeff.
